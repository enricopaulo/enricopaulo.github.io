Latest project

We worked on the photograph and online photo album for the wedding of Nick & Steph. The wedding was in a small picturesque town on the coast of Rhodes, the destination and wedding were both beautiful.

After considering the best way to showcase the photos to friends & family we came up with the idea of showing them online as opposed to a traditional physical photo album. The website is fully responsive and extremely simple to use, even for those older family members who aren't computer literate.
